How it Works

How it Works

Ritzcoin is your home for decentralized finance and digital asset trading. You can create, store, invest and manage any DeFi token with ease. You can choose from a range of liquidity pools cross-chain (Ethereum, Binance, etc.), seamlessly swap cryptocurrencies, and quickly perform bridge transactions. You can use ERC wallets for liquidity and bridge your STARS (ERC-20 compliant) tokens to BSC (Binance Smart Chain) and vice versa.

In addition, Ritzswap allows easy and quick crypto swapping, stake your funds into pools or create liquidity pairs to stake in farms.
